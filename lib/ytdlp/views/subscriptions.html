Subscriptions

Automatically download new videos from YouTube channels you subscribe to here. Downloaded media lands in your library like any other file -- deleting it in FileTube removes it everywhere, and it stays deleted (it will not be re-downloaded by a later check).

When off (default), members-only/subscriber-only videos are always skipped, regardless of this toggle.

One-off download

Download a single YouTube video once -- no subscription is created. Lands in a "One-Off" folder by default.
